Just signup and write down your app name!


Adding Users

Once a user fills in your app's registration form, make sure to get his cellphone number. Then just send Kalasag a PUT request from your server! Here's a curl example.

Make sure to fill in the following fields:

  • YOUR_CLIENT_ID - your app's id given by Kalasag
  • YOUR_SECRET_KEY - your secret ley given by Kalasag
  • USER_ID - a unique ID for your user. Can be any mix of numbers or letters
  • USER_NAME - your user's name. ex. Beato Bongco
  • USER_NUMBER - your user's phone number. Make sure it starts with 63! ex. 63926XXXXXXX
curl -X PUT \
-d 'name=USER_NAME&number=USER_NUMBER' \
{{ url_for('api_user', client_id="YOUR_CLIENT_ID", user_id='USER_ID', secret_key="YOUR_SECRET_KEY", _external=True) }}

Sample terminal output: 

{
    "id": "777", 
    "name": "BeatoBongco", 
    "number": "63926XXXXXXX"
}

Send a One Time Password

After doing your own login magic, send a one time password to your users through the API. You can do this with a simple POST request.

Make sure to fill in the following fields:

  • YOUR_CLIENT_ID - your app's id given by Kalasag
  • YOUR_SECRET_KEY - your secret ley given by Kalasag
  • USER_ID - a unique ID for your user. Can be any mix of numbers or letters
curl -X POST \
{{ url_for('api_otp_request', client_id="YOUR_CLIENT_ID", user_id='USER_ID', secret_key="YOUR_SECRET_KEY", _external=True) }}

Sample SMS output:

Code: 424242

Verify the One Time Password

Once the user receives the SMS with the one time password, you can verify it with Kalasag with another simple POST request.

Make sure to fill in the following fields:

  • ONE_TIME_PASSWORD - the code you received on your phone via SMS
  • YOUR_CLIENT_ID - your app's id given by Kalasag
  • YOUR_SECRET_KEY - your secret ley given by Kalasag
  • USER_ID - a unique ID for your user. Can be any mix of numbers or letters
curl -X POST \
  -d 'code=ONE_TIME_PASSWORD' \
{{ url_for('api_otp_validate', client_id="YOUR_CLIENT_ID", user_id='USER_ID', secret_key="YOUR_SECRET_KEY", _external=True) }}

Sample terminal output:

{
    "valid": true
}
